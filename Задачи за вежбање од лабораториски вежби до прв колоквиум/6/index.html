Travel itinerary

A travel itinerary is a structured plan outlining the details of a journey, typically organized by day or location. It serves as a roadmap for travel, guiding them through their trip with scheduled activities, accommodations, and transportation arrangements.

This itinerary allows travelers to efficiently manage their time, ensure they don't miss out on important experiences, and stay organized throughout their adventure
