Full Body Workout Routine

Here's a comprehensive full-body workout routine to keep you fit and healthy! Remember consistency is the key for progress. Start your journey towards a healthier lifestyle today!

Warm-up

  • Jogging (5 minutes) - Get your blood flowing with a light jog to warm up your muscles and increase your heart rate.
  • Jumping Jacks (3 sets of 20) - Jumping jacks are a great way to engage multiple muscle groups and improve cardiovascular endurance.
  • Arm Circles (2 sets of 10 each direction) - Loosen up your shoulder joints and improve flexibility with arm circles.
  • Leg Swings (2 sets of 10 each leg) - Dynamic leg swings help to improve hip mobility and prepare your legs for more intense exercises.
